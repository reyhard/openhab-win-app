B. Top bar button, sitemap expanded

Best split if we use this direction: the top bar toggles visibility, while sitemap selection and refresh move into the sitemap panel header.

Design read: this can work. I would avoid putting the sitemap selector itself in the top bar. The top bar should expose the sitemap as a mode/panel toggle; the expanded panel should own sitemap-specific controls.
